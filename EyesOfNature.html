Eyes Of Nature


How can I forget ?

How can I forget those eyes ?

In the chaos of thousands of them, mine only caught hers. I remember them so well, the ones I couldn’t blink while looking at. Soft light brown, the ones that have a loud shine in the dark, that turns sunlight into warmth. She has those eyes that whispers “slow down” in a room full of rush. And why rushing when those eyes are inviting me to a whole new world ? A world of nature, old books and music, a world where peace breathes softly.
They reminded me of coffee; brown, warm, deep. but now coffee reminds me of them, or perhaps, of her. She clearly doesn’t realize the power she has, on me at least. I admit !
And whenever I dare to look away, my gaze clings to her hair.. That brown hair finishing the fantasy her eyes had begun. Every curl tells a story as if they were alive, every movement takes my breath. I watched them falling, spiraling, dancing softly in the air. As if they were showing their gratitude to be a part of her. NADA, her name was… I forever thought that NADA means nothing (in Spanish) but after what I’ve seen and from now on, N-A-D-A means a lot… a lot more than my pen could write.
