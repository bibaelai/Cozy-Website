White Coffee Bean

Looking at my dark cup of coffee, i’m reminded of her deep, mysterious eyes. Initially, knowing her is like the bitterness of the first sip, it caught me off guard. But just as coffee gradually awakens my soul with each sip i take, she’s revealing a warmth that enveloped me entirely.
Addictives, she was.
I yearned for her just as a baby craves his mother’s milk when something’s off. The path i have with coffee is the same as hers, much like exploring uncharted lands hand in hand in her company, it’s as if we traversed a world painted in vibrant green and earthy browns. The gentle touch of the cup reminds me of her soft pinkish lips, a sensation that resonates deeply within.
She’s not just a drink; she’s an experience full of flavours and emotions that leaves me endlessly weak and comforted.
She’s my perfect cup of coffee.
